How to Play - Rook Mode

🎮 Game Basics

Rook Mode is moderately hard. Opponents play strategically and have decent stats. Training has risk - higher stats mean higher failure chance.

⚔️ Start Your Team

Choose your identity. Your core players start with 8 in your identity attribute. You earn fewer points than normal, so manage your training wisely.

📋 Matches

Opponents are moderately strong. You must choose attributes strategically. Each win/draw/loss gives different point rewards.

🏋️ Training

Training is risky. Stats at 13+ have 70% failure rate. Plan your training carefully. After 10 matches, redraft your squad.

📊 Progression

Complete seasons and build your team carefully. Strategy and planning are key to success in Rook Mode.

Resign from League?

This will permanently delete your squad, points, and all progress. This cannot be undone.
